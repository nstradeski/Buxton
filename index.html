Recommended

Mum stops at Leek

Lewes & Newcastle parties bring the dry goods. Mum stops at Morrisons Leek or Aldi Leek on her way in (~15 min off the A53, 10 min from the barnhouse) for the fresh stuff — her drive is shortest so it's the lowest-pain stop.

Bring from Lewes / Newcastle
  • 800g dried pasta · 4 tins plum tomatoes
  • Olive oil · salt · sugar · oregano · chilli flakes
  • Whole garlic bulbs
Mum picks up at Leek
  • 2 sourdough / ciabatta loaves
  • Fresh basil, parsley
  • Vegan butter (Flora Plant / Naturli)
  • Vegan parm (Violife)
  • Breakfast bits + kid snacks for Thu
✅ Long-haul parties drive direct · ✅ Mum's stop is a short detour

Click & collect, Morrisons Leek

Order online a day or two ahead, pick up a pre-booked slot at ~14:30 on Wed. No aisle wandering with tired kids.

How
  • Order via Morrisons or Tesco app
  • Pick a ~14:00–15:00 slot
  • Pay by card, drive up, boot loaded
✅ Fast · ❌ Need to book 24h+ ahead

Delivery to the barnhouse

Have the lot delivered ~17:00 on Wed straight to Gib Torr Farm. Best for full week's shopping if you're staying put.

How
  • Email host to confirm deliveries OK
  • Book Tesco / Asda / Sainsbury's for ~17:00–18:00
  • One adult home to receive
✅ Zero stops · ❌ Risky if slot slips · dinner late
